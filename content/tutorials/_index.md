<h2>Workshops</h2>

<p>
Workshops extend MayaFlux beyond tutorials.
They are not step-by-step instructions, but structured encounters with real contexts,
where sound, space, and computation are investigated together.
</p>

<p>
Each workshop documents a specific situation: participants, environment, constraints, etc..
and explores how MayaFlux can be used as a tool for inquiry rather than just production.
</p>

<p>
Unlike tutorials, workshops do not aim for API clarity.
They aim for observation, interpretation, and construction.
</p>
